<Update label="2026-04-06" description="v0.27.0" tags={["Release"]}>
  ## Refreshed site with new project pages and contact flow

  The Sonicverse website has been rebuilt with a clearer structure, updated branding, and new ways to get in touch.

  ### New features

  - **Project index** — A new [projects page](https://sonicverse.eu/projects) lets you browse all active Sonicverse projects in one place, with dedicated pages for each project.
  - **Improved contact form** — The contact page now routes bug reports and feature requests directly to GitHub Issues, so your feedback reaches the right team faster.
  - **Slack community** — You can now join the [Sonicverse community Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/sonicverse-oss/shared_invite/zt-3u969i5rr-cmfgEycFAi8V7Baj0uBx0A) to ask questions, share feedback, and connect with other users.

  ### Updates

  - The site has been refreshed with updated Sonicverse branding and clearer messaging throughout.
  - About, Contribute, and Contact pages now have more concise, action-oriented content.
  - The homepage now highlights [Audiostreaming Stack](/audiostreaming-stack/index) as the featured project.
  - Site content is now managed as local MDX files instead of a third-party CMS, improving page load times and reliability.

  **Full changelog**: [v0.27.0](https://github.com/sonicverse-eu/website/releases/tag/v0.27.0)
</Update>
